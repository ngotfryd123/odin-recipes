Egg White Grill Sandwich Copy Recipe

This is my own version of Chick-fil-a's egg white grill sandwich.

I enjoyed this breakfast sandwich so much from Chick-fil-a that I decided to make
my own version of it. I like it because it's very simple to make and it keeps me full for a long time.
It's a juicy grilled chicken breast sandwiched by an english muffin with melted american cheese and a grilled rounded egg white.

Ingredients:

  • 1 english muffin sliced in half
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 slice of american cheese
  • 1/4 cup of egg whites
  • salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Take the chicken breast and wash it. Dry it with paper towel.
    Season it with salt and pepper both sides. Place it on the grill medium heat
    grilling both sides until the chicken breast juices run clear.
  2. Place eggwhites in a small pot that is round and takes the shape of the muffin.
    Set it to low heat and take out when it has solidified.
  3. Place cheese on one half of the muffin and place the eggwhite on the other half
    with the chicken on top. Put the halves together and enjoy!
