Strawberry Banana Smoothie Recipe

A very simple smoothie recipe with only 3 ingredients!

This smoothie recipe is so simple it can be done in under 5 minutes.
All you need are strawberries, bananas, and ice to make this smoothie.
Not only is it super quick and simple, it's very tasty too!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strawberries* 150g
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup of ice

Steps:

  1. Wash the strawberries and cut them into halves
  2. Peel banana and cut it into two
  3. Put the strawberries, bananas and ice into blender
  4. Blend until smooth consistency
  5. Serve
